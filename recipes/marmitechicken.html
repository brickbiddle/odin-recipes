Marmite chicken

Love it or hate it, give this Marmite chicken a go for an umami hit

Ingredients
  • 500g Chicken Thighs
  • 100g Cornflour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp Marmite
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Cornflour slurry (mix 1 tbsp of cournflour with 2 tbsp water)
  • Optional - Spring onion
Method
  1. Slice your chicken thighs width ways into good chunks
  2. Coat your slices in a first layer of cornflour
  3. Whisk your egg
  4. Dip your chicken into the egg and then a second coating of cornflour
  5. Fry in vegetable oil until crisp (in batches if necessary)
  6. Remove from pan and leave on a wire rack to aid the crisping
  7. Mix your Soy, vinegar, honey and marmite and add an extra tbsp of water if too thick
  8. Dice your garlic
  9. Add your mixture and garlic into a pan with a dash of veg oil and bring up to a simmer
  10. Once simmering and your cornflour slurry to thicken
  11. Add your cripsy chicken back in and bring back up to heat coating in the sauce
  12. Transfer to a plate, great with rice and vegetables and some scattered spring onion
