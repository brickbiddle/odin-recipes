Cacio e pepe

The easiest, tastiest, cacio e pepe to grace your plates and pockets

Ingredients

  • 100g Pecorino cheese
  • 8 Black peppercorns
  • 160g Spaghetti

Method

  1. Get a saucepan full of water up to the boil and add a good helping of salt
  2. Add your Spaghetti and bring down to a simmer (use 1 minute less than package cooking time for Au Dente!)
  3. Lightly toast your peppercorns, keep an eye on them they burn easy!
  4. Bash your peppercorns down in a pestle and mortar
  5. Grate your pecorino and add to a bowl
  6. Get your smashed peppercorns back on the heat and add a ladel of pasta water from your spaghetti
  7. As your spaghetti nears cooking time, ladel some more pasta water over your pecorino and mix to a paste
  8. When spaghetti is just before Au Dente add to your peppercorn pan (Do not sieve, excess pasta water = good)
  9. Add in your pecorino paste and mix vigorously
  10. Take straight off the heat and enjoy immediately
