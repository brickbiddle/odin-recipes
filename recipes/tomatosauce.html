Tomato sauce

Just like Nonna used to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 x Good quality tinned tomatoes
  • 1 x Bunch basil
  • 4 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • Salt

Method

  1. Slice your garlic width ways as thin as you can
  2. Add your olive oil, basil and garlic to a frying pan - do not preheat the pan!
  3. Get the heat on low and slow until you start to smell garlic
  4. Add in your chopped toms and increase the heat until bubbling
  5. Bring the heat right back down and simmer until you're hungry
  6. (Optional) throw in a handful of parmesan towards the end
  7. Serve with your favourite pasta, bread, chips, your shout
